name: Rhythmic odd
source:
- "Arom S.: *African Polyphony and Polyrhythm*. Cambridge University Press, 1991"
- "Toussaint, G: *Rhytmic Oddity*. In: The Geometry of Musical Rhythm, chapter 15. Chapman and Hall, 2013"
---

These rhythms, first described by Simha Aron in the study of Aka pygmies music, cannot be split into two parts of equal length, both starting with a beat.
